Interview Preparation

Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)

DSA is the foundation of any technical interview. Here are some key topics to master:

  • Arrays & Strings
  • Linked Lists
  • Stacks & Queues
  • Trees & Graphs
  • Sorting & Searching Algorithms

Resume Building

Your resume is your first impression. Make sure it's perfect:

  • Use a clean, single-page template.
  • Highlight your projects with clear, quantifiable results.
  • List your skills, including programming languages and tools.
  • Proofread multiple times to avoid any typos.

Company-wise Questions

While every interview is different, many service-based companies ask similar types of questions:

  • Basic questions about your final year project.
  • Puzzles and aptitude questions.
  • Questions on basic concepts of C++, Java, and SQL.
  • "Tell me about yourself" and other common HR questions.
