Frequently Asked Questions

A few common questions you might have.

Is all the content on this site really free?

Yes, absolutely. All roadmaps, guides, and resources on this website are 100% free. My goal is to build a community, not to sell a course.

Who is this website for?

This site is built for Computer Science and IT students, or anyone who wants to become a web developer. It's especially for those who are tired of scattered tutorials and want a clear, step-by-step path.

Can I use your projects in my portfolio?

Yes! You are encouraged to build the projects from the "Project Ideas" section. I highly recommend adding your own unique features to them to make them stand out, and then add them to your own portfolio.
